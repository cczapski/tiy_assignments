For Future + Current Volunteers

As a WorldTeach Volunteer, you are:

  • Between the ages of 18 and 74 at the time of departure
  • Committing to 3 month or year-long durations (and holding a bachelor's degree for year-long programs)
  • Selecting where out of the 17 partner countries you'd like to teach
  • Responsible for some program costs
  • Flexible, open-minded, and patient

Why WorldTeach?

Choose Your Destination

One of the great perks about WorldTeach is that you get to choose your country of service. Explore our partner country world and learn more about the exciting destinations you can serve and discover.

Meet Our Volunteers

We believe our volunteers speak best to our program, the people we help, and the wonderful experience you embark on during your time with WorldTeach. Let us introduce you to current and past volunteers for each destination.

Program Costs

To establish trust and credibility with our volunteers and donors, we believe in full disclosure surrounding any costs associated with service. We break down where every dollar requested of you goes, and also show you how you can fundraise every penny.

How It Works

Planning any trip abroad is hugely overwhelming, and one of our main goals is to prepare you for everything. We outline the full timeline from your research right now all the way up to your return to home after your service.
