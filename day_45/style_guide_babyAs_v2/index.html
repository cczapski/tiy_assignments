Goals

Baby Acapulco's, or Baby A's, is a local tex-mex staple in Austin, surpassing 30 years as a family friendly restaurant with 5 convenient locations. Their fruity drinks, or "celebrities", are famous attractions, as well as their giant bowls of queso and delicious tortilla soup. Diners range from Austinites, to travelers, to families, to college students, virtually to almost any variety of patron. Baby A's has undergone many menu, logo and restaurant designs over the years.

With the almost daily increase in Austin's population (check out Forbes' prediction of Austin with the highest projected economic growth rate in the US from 2011 - 2016 here) also comes a dramatic increase in the number of new restaurants entering an already populated market. Against this sea of new choices for dining, Baby A's faces the challenges of:

  • Retaining it's existing customer base
  • Attracting the new Austin community
  • Standing out to travelers and tourists as a worthy choice for dining

This website redesign project centers around addressing the current market challenges and focuses on two key website structure adjustments detailed below:

  • Updating all content on the site to exist outside of gif files for ease in readibility and accessibility extending past only visual access
  • Updating the responsive ability across all aspects of the site for optimum viewing on any device

With these adjustments, the new design will maintain a refreshing style that emulates the bold and casual environment of the existing Baby Acapulco's.

Typography

FF Enzo by FontFont through Adobe Typekit is the single typeface used in the redesign. This type was chosen in order to create a more modern and fun feel to the website with its san-serif and small, human-like characteristics. FF Enzo is also very versatile with its various weights and styles, providing the ability for variation in a single typeface.

Grid

The site has a simple 12 column grid layout.

Small

For any device smaller than 560px, all elements on a page take up 100% width.

Width 100%, Margin 1em

Medium

For a medium sized device, the content follows the layout below with the focus content taking up the majority of the page.

Width 33%, Margin 1em

Width 66%, Margin 1em

Large

Any device with a width above 960px expands to a three-column grid option where possible (i.e. the menu pages) and maintains the medium grid layout when a third column isn't appropriate for the content on the page. To maintain readibility for very large screen sizes, a maximum width of 1300px is set.

Navigation

The site has three different types of navigation to aid in accessibility across different areas in the site.

Main Navigation

The main navigation appears across the top right of the page for medium to large sized devices. This location was chosen to maintain a level of familiarity on navigating the main sections of the website.

  • Food + Drink
  • Locations
  • Events
  • Catering/Parties

Secondary Navigation

The secondary navigation appears on the home page and each of the menu sections of the website. With such a large amount of menu content, this navigation allows easier accessiblity to the different food genres without having to navigate to another page.

  • Happy Hour
  • Drinks
  • Lunch Specials
  • Appetizers

Footer Navigation

The footer navigation appears within the footer of the website on every page, consistent with common footer navigation practices. The main navigation pages are included, as well as the less frequented pages not accessible anywhere else in the site.

  • Food + Drink
  • Locations
  • Events
  • Catering/Parties
  • About
  • Gift Cards
  • Jobs
